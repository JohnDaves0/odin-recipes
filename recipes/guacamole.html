Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a creamy, flavorful dip made primarily from ripe avocados, often seasoned with lime juice, onions, tomatoes, and fresh herbs. Originally from Mexico, it has become a popular snack and appetizer around the world, served with tortilla chips or alongside tacos, burritos, and grilled meats. Its freshness and rich texture make it a crowd-pleasing favorite.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin (optional)
  • ½ cup diced red onion
  • 1–2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium tomato, diced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 1 small jalapeño, seeded and minced (optional)

Steps

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pits, and scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  2. Mash the avocados with a fork or potato masher until smooth or slightly chunky, depending on preference.
  3. Add lime juice, salt, and cumin, and mix to combine.
  4. Stir in diced onion, garlic, tomato, cilantro, and jalapeño.
  5. Taste and adjust seasoning as needed (more salt, lime, or heat).
  6. Cover with plastic wrap directly on the surface to prevent browning and refrigerate until ready to serve.
  7. Serve with tortilla chips, tacos, or as a topping for grilled dishes.
